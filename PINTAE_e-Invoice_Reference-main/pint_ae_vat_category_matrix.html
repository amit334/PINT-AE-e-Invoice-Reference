Key schematron rules governing this matrix
ibr-122-aeDoc types 480 and 81 must only use E, O, or Z. S, AE, and N are forbidden.
ibr-151-aeDoc types 380 and 381 must have at least one line with a VAT category other than E or O.

ibr-103-aeWhen VAT = AE (Reverse Charge), Buyer VAT identifier IBT-048 must be provided.
